HOW CAN YOU CONTRIBUTE?

Since the club, as well as the conference, are entirely student-driven, every year, we hunt for students who believe in the ideology of TED- who believe that ideas can truly change the world. For the same, we have our Volunteer Program for TEDxShivNadarUniversity 2019, scheduled to take place in November. TEDxShivNadarUniversity is the Herculean project the club undertakes every year. The conference hosts speakers coming from every sphere and part of the world. We will soon be taking in volunteers for the following teams:

  1. Content and Curation
  2. Marketing
  3. Design
  4. Decor
  5. Videography
  6. Sponsorship
  7. Web Development

Keep an eye out for our recruitment forms!
